How to be Proper

Basic Etiquette to greet a lady

  • Wait for her acknowledging bow.
  • Use your right hand to greet her.
  • Walk with her if she wishes to begin a conversation.
  • When walking, the lady must always have the wall.

Resource for a lady toward unpleasant men who will not leave her

  1. A stare of iciness should work most of the times.
  2. A cold bow discourages familiarity.
  3. A last resort. "Sir, I have not the honor of your aquaintance."

Proper Address of Royalty

Your Majesty
To the King or the Queen.
Your Royal Highness
To the Monarch's Spouse, children, and siblings
Your Highness
To nephews, niecesm and cousins of the sovereign.
